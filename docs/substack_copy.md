## About page

US Stock Monitor ranks all ~500 S&P 500 companies every week using two factors
with a long, well-documented track record in academic research:

- **Price momentum** — stocks trending up over the last 6 months.
- **Fundamental growth** — revenue and earnings growth from official SEC filings.

Each week I publish the model's top picks, what changed in the rankings, and which
names report earnings soon. A free weekly summary keeps you in the loop; the
**Premium** digest gives you the full top 10 with scores, prices, analyst targets,
every ranking change, and earnings alerts.

This is a **research and screening tool** — a starting point for your own homework,
not personalized financial advice. I share a systematic model, transparently. You
make and execute your own decisions.

**Free:** weekly summary. **Premium ($7/mo or $60/yr):** full rankings + changes +
earnings alerts, every week.

## Welcome post / first email

**Subject:** Welcome to US Stock Monitor 📈

Thanks for subscribing. Here's what you'll get:

Every week, I run a momentum + growth model across the entire S&P 500 and send you
the results — the strongest names, what moved in the rankings, and which top picks
have earnings coming up.

Why these two signals? Because momentum and growth are among the most durable,
research-backed factors in equity markets — and because I'd rather show you a
transparent, rules-based model than vague "hot tips."

**Free subscribers** get a weekly summary. **Premium subscribers** get the full
top 10 with scores, prices, analyst targets, every ranking change, and earnings
alerts — the complete picture to do your own research with.

A quick, important note: this is a research tool, **not investment advice**, and
nothing here is personalized to your situation. Rankings and past performance don't
guarantee future results. Always do your own due diligence, and only invest what
you can afford to lose.

Glad to have you here.
